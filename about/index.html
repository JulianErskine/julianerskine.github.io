About Me

I am currently doing my PhD in Robotics with the ARMEN group of the Laboratoire des Science du Numerique de Nantes (LS2N) at the Ecole Centrale de Nantes (ECN), France. I anticipate defending my thesis entitled Control of Dynamic Heterogeneous Drone Swarms in the fall of 2021.

I have studied at ECN since I began my M.Sc in Advanced Robotics (2016-2018), following my graduation with a B.Eng in Mechanical Engineering from Dalhousie University in Halifax, Canada (2011-2016).
